Drug Eggs

Mayak eggs are often times called Drug eggs because of how addicting they are. Hard boiled eggs are already a fun little snack or toppings that you can in many dishes. Why not spice them up with some great flavor.

Ingredients

  • 6 large eggs
  • 1 tbsp each salt and white vinegar
  • green onions (seaweed flakes and sesame oil optional)

    • Marinade

  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 1/4 medium onion
  • 1 green onion
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 2 chili peppers
  • 1 tbsp toasted sesame seeds

Lets Cook

  1. In a bowl, add marinade ingredients and mix
  2. Add vinegar and salt to a pot of boiling water
  3. Reduce heat and add eggs. Simmer for 6 minutes
  4. Immediately transfer eggs to an ice bath for 5-10 minutes
  5. Peel and add the eggs to the marinade. Cover and store in fridge over night
  6. Serve over a bed of rice and add garnish
