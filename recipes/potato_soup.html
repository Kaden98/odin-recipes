Potato Soup

On hood we got some soup of the potato flavor. This soup is so fire especially on a cold rainy night. While it isn't the healthies of meals, it still gets the job done and you will have one HECK of a time scarffin this down the hatch. This stuff look

Ingredients

  • 6 Strips of bacon
  • 3 Tablespoons of buter
  • 1 Yellow Onion
  • 3 Cloves of garlic
  • 1/3 cups of all purpose flour
  • 2 1/2 pounds of potatoes
  • 4 cups of chicken broth
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 2/3 heavy cream
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground pepper
  • 1/4 - 1/2 teaspoon ancho chili powder
  • 2/3 cup sour cream
  • Chives, Bacon, Shredded cheese, and Sour Cream for toppings

Let's Cook

  1. Cook the bacon in a Dutch Oven over Medium heat until it's cripsy brown
  2. Set the bacon aside while leaving the fat in the pot
  3. Add the butter and chopped onion and cook over medium heat until tender (3-5 minutes)
  4. Add Garlic and cook for roughly 30 seconds
  5. Sprinkle in the flour and stir until smooth
  6. Add in Diced potatoes, chicken broth, milk, heavy cream, salt, pepper, and chili powder and stir well
  7. Bring to a boil until potatoes are tender (about 10 minutes)
  8. Reduce to a simmer and puree half of the soup until smooth
  9. Return pureed soup to the pot and add sour cream and reserved bacon pieces
  10. Simmer for 15 minutes before serving
  11. Top with sour crea, bacon, cheddar cheese, and/or chives
